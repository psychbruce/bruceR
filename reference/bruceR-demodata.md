# Demo data.

Demo datasets of multi-factor ANOVA as examples to show how the
functions
[`MANOVA()`](https://psychbruce.github.io/bruceR/reference/MANOVA.md)
and
[`EMMEANS()`](https://psychbruce.github.io/bruceR/reference/EMMEANS.md)
work.

## Format

- **1. Between-Subjects Design**:

  - `between.1` - A(4)

  - `between.2` - A(2) \* B(3)

  - `between.3` - A(2) \* B(2) \* C(2)

- **2. Within-Subjects Design**:

  - `within.1` - A(4)

  - `within.2` - A(2) \* B(3)

  - `within.3` - A(2) \* B(2) \* C(2)

- **3. Mixed Design**:

  - `mixed.2_1b1w` - A(2, between) \* B(3, within)

  - `mixed.3_1b2w` - A(2, between) \* B(2, within) \* C(2, within)

  - `mixed.3_2b1w` - A(2, between) \* B(2, within) \* C(2, between)

## Source
